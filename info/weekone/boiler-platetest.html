Hi, my name is Jasmin!

Jasmin Rose Guerrera is a Canadian designer and animator. While completing her bachelor degree in Film Animation at Concordia University, she worked for the Concordia Film Festival's technical team helping woth DCP distribution and at Spectrum Productions where she created multiple films.

She believes in making comics, films and animations that will get audiences to react and to feel emotions strongly. Jasmin believes people remember the emotions they felt during a past event more than the event itself. Therefore, her main intention with her work is to get the audience to emotionally react as she believes that is the most memorable part an artistic process can evoke.
