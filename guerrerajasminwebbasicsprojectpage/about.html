About the Cartoonist:

ADD LINK TO THIS IMAGE!!!!!!! Jasmin Rose Guerrera is a Canadian artist and filmmaker from Montreal Quebec. She recently completed her undergraduate degree from Concordia University Mel Hoppenheim School of Cinema with an undergraduate in Film Animation. She believes in making comics, films and animations that will get an audience to react and to feel emotions strongly. I believe people remember the emotions they felt during a past event more than the event itself. Therefore, my main intention with my work is to get the audience to emotionally react as I believe that is the most memorable part an artistic process can evoke.
