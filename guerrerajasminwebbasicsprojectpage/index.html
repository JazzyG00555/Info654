About the comic:

The Marge Mania is a slice of life type of comic strip. It is full of fun and simple gags revolving around the life of Marge and her hatred math. She is just a kid trying to get through life. Even though life literally seems impossible all the time. There are many characters within the comic strip as well. This includes a math teacher named Ms. Larock, a best friend named Jewels, an Italian grandmother named Nonna Rosina and multitutde of other friends!
